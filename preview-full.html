Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

Mi Ritmo Digital is developed for academic research purposes. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use the app as part of our research study.

Data We Collect

The app collects only the following information:

  • App usage statistics: Which apps you use and for how long
  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, and a unique device identifier
  • App interaction data: How you interact with Mi Ritmo Digital features

We do not collect any other data. The app does not access your location, contacts, messages, photos, browsing history, or any personal content on your device.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for:

  • Displaying your usage statistics within the app
  • Enforcing usage budgets you have set
  • Academic research on smartphone usage patterns

Data Storage and Security

Your data is:

  • Transmitted securely to our research server using encryption
  • Stored on secure servers with restricted access
  • Associated with a de-identified participant code, not your name or email
  • Accessible only to authorized members of the research team

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Anonymized, aggregated data may be used in academic publications.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Withdraw from the study at any time
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request a copy of your data

Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Usage Access: To track which apps you use and for how long
  • Display over other apps: To show usage warnings when you reach your budget limits

Young People's Privacy

This app may be used by participants aged 13 to 17 as part of our research study. For participants under 18, we require signed parental or guardian consent before any data is collected.

Parents and guardians have the right to:

  • Review the data collected from their child
  • Request deletion of their child's data at any time
  • Withdraw their child from the study

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Data Retention

Identifiable data is deleted when the study ends or when you withdraw from the study. Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for up to 5 years for research purposes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact:

Data Deletion Request

How to request deletion of your data

Your Right to Data Deletion

As a participant in our research study, you have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time. This page explains what data we collect, what gets deleted, and how to submit a request.

Data We Collect

Mi Ritmo Digital collects the following types of data:

  • Email address: Used only during enrollment to link you to your participant code
  • De-identified participant ID: A unique code that identifies your data without revealing your identity
  • App usage statistics: Which apps you use and for how long
  • App interaction data: How you interact with Phone Dashboard features
  • Crash logs: Technical information collected via Microsoft AppCenter when the app encounters errors

What Gets Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your email address from our enrollment records
  • All app usage data linked to your participant ID
  • Your participant profile and settings
  • Any crash logs associated with your device

What May Be Retained

The following data may be retained after a deletion request:

  • Anonymized aggregate data: Statistical summaries that cannot be linked back to you
  • Research records: Documentation required for research integrity and audit purposes

How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please:

  1. Send an email to the address below with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include your participant ID or the email address you used to enroll
  3. State that you wish to have your data deleted

We will process your request within 30 days and send you confirmation once your data has been deleted.

Contact

Send your data deletion request to:

Duncan Webb
Nova School of Business and Economics
duncan.webb@novasbe.pt

Please use the subject line: Data Deletion Request - Mi Ritmo Digital

After Deletion

Once your data is deleted, you will no longer be able to participate in the study. If you only wish to stop using the app but keep your data for the research, you can simply uninstall the app without submitting a deletion request.
